Reflection on Professional Conduct

This page reflects on my own professional conduct throughout the module and beyond, considering the influence of the BCS (British Computer Society) code of conduct.

My Professional Conduct

Throughout the module and beyond, I have strived to maintain integrity by adhering to ethical guidelines in all my endeavors. Upholding the principles outlined in the BCS code of conduct has been paramount in guiding my behavior and decision-making processes.

However, upon reflection, I recognize the need to enhance my engagement in team projects and refine my communication skills. While I have contributed effectively to individual tasks, fostering greater collaboration within teams and improving communication methods are areas where I aim to make significant improvements.

By actively participating in team discussions, offering support to colleagues, and refining my communication techniques to ensure clarity and conciseness, I aspire to foster a more cohesive and productive team environment.

This commitment to enhancing team collaboration and communication aligns with my overarching goal of continually refining my professional conduct to achieve optimal outcomes in my career pursuits.
